About This Demo

This is a simple example showing how easy it is to add the BrainFish widget to any website. The swimming fish at the top of the page represent tasks from the BrainFish macOS productivity app.

Try moving your mouse near the fish - they'll swim away faster when you get close, just like in the desktop app!
